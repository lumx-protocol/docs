**Well done!** You've accomplished a significant milestone with your first project!

Let's take a moment to celebrate what we've achieved and outline our next steps.

Firstly, we obtained our API key using the [create a project](/api-reference/v2/projects/create-a-project.mdx) endpoint. This key is now included in every subsequent request, ensuring smooth communication with the API.

Next, we breathed life into your inaugural contract. We followed three crucial steps: [create a contract](/api-reference/v2/contracts/create-a-contract.mdx), [create a token type](/api-reference/v2/contracts/create-an-item-type.mdx), and [deploy a contract](/api-reference/v2/contracts/deploy-a-contract.mdx). Remember these steps; they form the foundation for generating any contract within the API.

Then, we established your first wallet effortlessly using the [create a wallet](/api-reference/v2/wallets/create-a-wallet.mdx) endpoint. Simple, right?

Lastly, but certainly not least, we minted your inaugural token with the [mint a token](/api-reference/v2/transactions/mint-tokens.mdx) endpoint. Cherish this first token with care!

Looking ahead, you have two exciting options. You can opt for our Next.js boilerplate using our CLI to swiftly create a pre-built application. Alternatively, delve into our recipes to discover the endless possibilities with Lumx Protocol, and seamlessly integrate them into your own application.
